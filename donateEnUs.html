Why and to whom to donate?

By donating on our website you can support and help environmental NGOs to maintain Projects that will save the planet, you will See the difference all around you! A small amount can make all the difference in the world.

In the dictionary, to donate means "to transfer freely to another person, in a legal way, well, amount, property or advantages, etc., which were formerly in his possession."

But we, at Future in Action, know that giving is much more than Right. The act of donating effectively contributes to the improvement of society, institutions and, above all, the people. To By seeing the needs of others and doing something to meet them, we become more just and Equal.
