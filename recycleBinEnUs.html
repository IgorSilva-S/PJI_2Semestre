How to play?

Click on the trash that is on your screen, when you click it, it will be selected.

When selecting (grabbing) the trash, click on the correct trash can from it. Each garbage placed in a correct bin gives two points, in the wrong ones, you lose 3 points

Your score can be seen in the scoring part, below the trash viewer.


If the garbage appears in a place where it is difficult to pick it up, press the dotted reset, thus, placing the same garbage in another location
